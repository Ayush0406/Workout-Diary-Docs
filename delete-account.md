# Account Deletion Request for WorkoutDiary

We respect your privacy and give you full control over your data. You can delete your account and all associated workout data at any time using either of the methods below.

### Method 1: Delete from within the app (Fastest)
If you currently have the WorkoutDiary app installed on your device, you can instantly and permanently delete your account:
1. Open the WorkoutDiary app.
2. Navigate to the **Profile Screen**.
3. Tap on **Delete Account** and confirm your choice.
*Note: This action is irreversible and will instantly wipe your account credentials and all logged workout data.*

### Method 2: Request deletion via email (If app is uninstalled)
If you no longer have the app installed and wish to have your data deleted, you can submit a manual web request:
1. Send an email to **berserkdesserter@gmail.com**.
2. You **must** send the email from the exact email address you used to register your WorkoutDiary account.
3. Use the subject line: **"Account Deletion Request"**.

Once we receive your verified request, we will manually wipe your account credentials and any associated workout data from our database within 7 days. You will receive a final confirmation email once the deletion is complete.
